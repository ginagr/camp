Payment and Fee Options

We want to make Camp accessible to all and offer camp fees on a sliding scale. In order to accommodate those who need to pay at the lowest rate and those who need campership assistance, we ask that you please pay at the middle or high rate if at all possible.

Pay Levels

We offer three pay levels for Summer Camp – choose which of these works best for you. Here are suggested guidelines for who pays which rate. We encourage you to pay the most you can so that we can reserve 25% of our spaces for those who can only afford the lowest rate:

Low rate: Household income of less than $70,000
Medium rate: Household income of $70,000 – $100,000
High rate: Household income of $100,000 and above

Financial Assistance/Work Exchanges

We are able to offer limited assistance (reduced camper fees) to families in financial need for Summer Camp. Campership funds are limited, but we will do our best to enable camp attendance. A small number of work exchanges are available (i.e. leading “specialist” programs in exchange for reduced fees).
