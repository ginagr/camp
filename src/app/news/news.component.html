Don’t have kids? Don’t worry!

There’s no need for you to miss out on the family camp experience. We have new programming for adults who come to camp on their own. Come play!


We are excited to announce that we are expanding our adult offerings and extending a special invitation to camp alumnae and friends to visit and support Camp It Up 2020. This is the first year that we have created programming especially for people who either don't have kids or whose kids don’t come to camp anymore, but who still enjoy the ambiance and community of camp. We want you to continue to shape the future of this much-loved space, to bring your friends and to come together to make beautiful safe space for all kinds of families. Yes, we have always offered adult programming, but this year we will have enhanced adult programming geared specifically for you!

What's New?

Camaraderie and Inclusion: We are embarking on the challenge of making camp an inclusive space for diverse community. We will have a People of Color Affinity Group lead by Chiah Connelly Ingram (who has been at camp for many years. Co-leaders are WELCOME!). There will also be an Allies Group. Again, possible co-leaders should contact Andrea or Jill to become part of the weeks planning.

The Space: We are considering clustering folks who come to camp without kids in the area closest to the entrance of camp (near the horses.) This way you can come and go a bit more easily without disturbing others or having to engage with other camp activities. (And, of course, you can still stay in whatever part of camp you most love but we do want to do a special space with difference ambiance for adult campers only.)

Adults Lead Workshops and Fun New Activities​: There is so much to do in beautiful Quincy and on the gorgeous Feather River that our list of potential activities is too long! We are hoping that folks in through the CIU Alumnae door will share knowledge and possibly lead an activity or workshop.

What We Are Offering:

You can choose one of two sessions: July 27-31 or July 31 to Aug 4 or you can stay for the whole week!​ ​You are welcome to participate in all Camp It Up! activities​ ​but we just wanted to give adults an extra chance to build community and friendships. Just like we have kids groups, now we can have adult groups!
